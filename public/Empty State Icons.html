Empty state icons · Field notes

Eight illustrations to replace the emoji in your empty states.

Same Field-notes vocabulary as the categories — single primary stroke, occasional secondary mark at 0.65 opacity for personality. Sized to read at 40–80px (empty-state hero size). All currentColor; will inherit whatever slate / teal tone the parent text uses. Naming follows the i-empty-* pattern so they slot cleanly into your existing sprite.

Notes:
  • i-empty-trip reuses the paper plane (matches i-transport) but adds a dashed curved trail to suggest "departure / journey starting".
  • i-empty-itinerary is a folded paper map with crease lines and a dashed route ending at a destination pin — more "field notes" than a generic map.
  • i-empty-crew is three heads in a row (vs i-group's two) — bigger group feel, suggests "the whole crew".
  • i-empty-split uses two distinct figures with a dashed split mark between them, reinforcing "share/divide" rather than "group".
  • i-empty-settled intentionally avoids confetti or fireworks — a clean check-in-circle with small sparks, calm rather than loud. Pair it with the --success emerald color in production for a quiet celebration.
